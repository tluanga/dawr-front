Features of Point of Sale Module
-Customer Selection
    - Customer can be existing customer or a new one or blank
-Product Selection
    - Selecting Product which are available in Stock
    - Those which are not available are screen out are not available for selection
-Data entry- 
    - Quantity
    - Discount
-Buttons-
    1) Add to Cart - Add the Item Selected to the Cart
    2) Clear- Clear the data entry

-Cart
    - Cart will compose of 
    -Item Table-
        Columns- Sl.No-Name-Quantity-Rate-gst-discount-Amount
        Footer-Total Rate, Total Gst, total discount and Total Amount
        Cart will contain a list of items added to cart
    - Button -
        -Print-Print the invoice
        -Clear -clear the cart
